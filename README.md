# Future Mom Rev - Holistic CEO Dashboard

A personalized, holistic dashboard for Shafira, CEO of Cetalabs, designed to harmonize business growth with future motherhood and personal well-being. This project serves as a "Company of One" operating system, integrating cycle syncing, life stage planning, and financial modeling.

## üåü Vision
To create a sustainable, joyful, and productive rhythm that adapts to biological needs and life chapters, ensuring that business success does not come at the cost of health or family.

## üìÇ Key Modules

### 1. [Holistic Dashboard (`index.html`)](index.html)
The central command center.
*   **Cycle Syncing:** tracks the menstrual cycle (Menstrual, Follicular, Ovulatory, Luteal) and adjusts daily schedules and task recommendations based on energy levels.
*   **Life Stage Selector:** Tailors OKRs and focus areas for different chapters: *Preparing for Pregnancy*, *Pregnancy*, *Parenting*, and *Parenting + Pregnant*.
*   **Season Modes:** Switches between *Nurture* (Conservative), *Balance* (Moderate), and *Sprint* (Ambitious) operating modes.
*   **Department OKRs:** visualizes goals across Self, Marriage, Business, Visibility, and Impact.

### 2. [Revenue & Retirement Planner (`slider.html`)](slider.html)
A financial simulation tool.
*   **Revenue Model:** Simulates income from 4 streams: Custom Dev, Training, Family AI, and Corporate.
*   **Growth Scenarios:** Toggles between Conservative, Moderate, and Ambitious revenue targets.
*   **Retirement Calculator:** Projects financial freedom based on savings rates, investment returns, and "soft retirement" plans.

### 3. [OKR & Routine Planner (`okr.html`)](okr.html)
A detailed breakdown of the operating modes.
*   **Energy Meter:** Visualizes the energy cost of each work scenario.
*   **Daily Rhythm:** Provides a template schedule for the ideal work week.
*   **Routine Checklists:** Weekly and monthly protocols to maintain the system.
